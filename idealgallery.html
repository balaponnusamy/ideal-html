about us

Our school campus is situated in a serene atmosphere between Anthiyur & Bhavani Main Road - 30 minute drive from Bhavani or a 10 Minute drive from Anthiyur. The green fields are lush pastures add beauty to the environment. It is away from the hustle and bustle of traffic and city life. This serenity is on the basis of 'Resident Learning System' under a saint.
